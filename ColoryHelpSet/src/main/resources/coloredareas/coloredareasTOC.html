Colored areas

At the top of Colory you find the colored areas to be pushed to repeat a sequence. The areas surround the 'Start' button. See the image below:
Each colored area has a different piano sound. On click the sound is played. The assignment of piano sounds is as follows:
Blue c
Yellow e
Green g
Red h

You can also use the keyboard keys c, e, g and h to repeat the sequences.
